We define \(X_i = [a,b] := X_i = I_{[0,1]}/(b-a)\) the indicator RV over \([0,1]\) normalized such that it integrates to 1.

\[\begin{aligned} X_1 & = [0,1] \\ X_2, X_3 & = [0,1/2), [1/2,1] \\ X_4,X_5,X_6 & = [0,1/3), [1/3,2/3), [2/3,1] \\ \dots \end{aligned}\]

That is we can imagine a box that is shrinking in width while increasing in height.
